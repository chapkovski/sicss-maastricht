There are two different participant roles: Participant A and Participant B.
Both participants, A and B, receive an initial endowment of {{ player.endowment }} at the beginning of the interaction. Then Participant A makes a single decision to transfer part of the initial endowment to Participant B.
An amount transferred by Participant A to Participant B will be multiplied by {{ Constants.coef }}. If, for example, Participant A transfers {{ 4|c }}, Participant B will receive {{ 12|c }}, in addition to his initial endowment.
Participant B will be informed of the decision of Participant A after he or she has made it. Participant B then has the possibility of transferring any amount of the received sum back to Participant A. This amount will not be multiplied for the back transfer, meaning that Participant A will receive exactly the amount that Participant B transfers back to him or her.
